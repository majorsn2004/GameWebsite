About this Website

For my college course, Interactive & Algorithmic Art, I created a retro game website as a creative project. The website showcases classic-style games with simple graphics, engaging gameplay, and it is designed to bring back a feeling of nostalgia. I created it to practice using art and coding together and to make a fun, interactive experience.
